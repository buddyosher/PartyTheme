1. Caprese Skewers (Kevin & Zach - Beers & Cheers to FIFTY)

🥘 Ingredients:

  • balsamic glaze

2. Buffalo Chicken Wings (Kevin & Zach - Beers & Cheers to FIFTY)

🥘 Ingredients:
  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 1/2 cup butter
👨‍🍳 Instructions:
  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Step 2: In a large bowl, mix chicken wings with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Step 3: Spread wings on a baking sheet and bake for 40-45 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  4. Step 4: In a saucepan, melt butter and mix with hot sauce.
  5. Step 5: Toss baked wings in the sauce before serving.

4. Loaded Nachos (Kevin & Zach - Beers & Cheers to FIFTY)

🥘 Ingredients:
  • 1 bag tortilla chips
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • 1 can sliced jalapeños
  • 1/2 cup black olives
  • 1 can refried beans
  • salsa
  • guacamole
  • sour cream
👨‍🍳 Instructions:
  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Step 2: Spread tortilla chips on a baking tray.
  3. Step 3: Top with shredded cheese, jalapeños, olives, and beans.
  4. Step 4: Bake for 10-15 minutes until cheese is melted.
  5. Step 5: Serve with salsa, guacamole, and sour cream.

5. Stuffed Mushrooms (Kevin & Zach - Beers & Cheers to FIFTY)

🥘 Ingredients:
  • 16 large mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • salt
  • pepper
  • parsley
👨‍🍳 Instructions:
  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Step 2: Remove stems from mushrooms and chop them finely.
  3. Step 3: In a skillet, sauté chopped stems, garlic, and onion until soft.
  4. Step 4: Mix in cream cheese and seasonings.
  5. Step 5: Stuff the mushroom caps with the mixture and bake for 20 minutes.
